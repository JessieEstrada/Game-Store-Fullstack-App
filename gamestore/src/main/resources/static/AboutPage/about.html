About Us:

Welcome to our Game Store, an online marketplace designed and developed out of a Netflix Bootcamp project. Initiated by three dedicated developers, our team's mission was to create an intuitive game store API using Spring JPA. Continuing past the bootcamp, two of us took the project further by developing this website, providing a seamless user experience for simulating product purchases. We're currently working to implement real payment methods, demonstrating our commitment to providing a comprehensive gaming e-commerce platform. Our Game Store is a testament to our expertise, passion, and determination. As you explore our site, we hope you appreciate our commitment to quality gaming and development. Enjoy!
